JSE - Pool Mining Experiment

To be able to also reward Steemeum users with JSE coins, this web based miner can be used to fill a JSE coin pool, and the results of this experiment could lead to further implementing JSE into Steemeum.

After a delay of 15 seconds you should start seeing JSE hashing data coming in via the the developer console. To open the developer console press CTRL + SHIFT + K in Firefox or CTRL + SHIFT + J in Chrome and Edge.
