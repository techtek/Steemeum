Features

Easy to use mobile app

The quality and usability of the app is guaranteed by a wide-ranging beta test with more than 100 users.

Proof of Time mechanism

Mining is based on Proof of Time. Are you committed and competitive enough to collect the most mining-time?

Weekly Payouts

Every Sunday 12am (UTC) the weekly rewards pool is split and transfered to the users. The mining-time will be reset and all users have equal chances for the next week.

Rewards pool

The reward pool consists of rewards made by Steemeum on the Steem Blockchain with Fundition, and of the rewards from the users that upvote the daily miner and statistics posts

Steem Blockchain

To use Steemeum you need to have a Steem account. With your Steem account you can get rewarded with the Steem cryptocurrency for a wide variety of activities such as blogging, rating and creating content, play games, watching videos and much more.
