JSE - Pool Mining Experiment v0.0.2

To be able to also reward Steemeum users with JSE coins, this web based miner can be used to fill a JSE coin pool, and the results of this experiment could lead to further implementing JSE into Steemeum.

Mine with this app JSE Coins for the Steemeum miner pool, open it a few times a day and keep it mining for a few minutes.
